Causes:

    Diabetes is caused by problems with insulin, the hormone that regulates blood sugar levels. Type 1 diabetes is an autoimmune disease where the body attacks and destroys insulin-producing cells, while type 2 diabetes is caused by a combination of genetic and lifestyle factors (such as obesity and inactivity).

Treatments:

    Treatment for diabetes may include medication (such as insulin or oral medications), lifestyle changes (such as a healthy diet and exercise), and/or insulin pump therapy.

Medications:

    Common medications for diabetes include insulin, metformin, sulfonylureas, and GLP-1 receptor agonists.

Medical Advice:

    To manage diabetes, it is important to maintain a healthy lifestyle, including a balanced diet, regular exercise, monitoring blood sugar levels, taking medication as prescribed, and managing any other health conditions that may affect blood sugar levels.
